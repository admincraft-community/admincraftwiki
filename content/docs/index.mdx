Welcome to the Admincraft Wiki, your definitive resource for Minecraft server administration. Whether you're launching your first server or managing a established community, you'll find the knowledge and tools you need to succeed.

## About This Wiki

The Admincraft Wiki is a living document maintained by experienced server administrators from the [Admincraft community](https://www.reddit.com/r/admincraft/). Our goal is to provide:

- **Comprehensive Guides**: From basic setup to advanced optimization
- **Best Practices**: Security, performance, and community management
- **Troubleshooting**: Solutions to common issues and challenges
- **Community Knowledge**: Insights from experienced administrators


## Join Our Community

The Admincraft Wiki is a collaborative effort, and we're always growing. You can:

- [Contribute](contribution.mdx) your knowledge to help others
- Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/admincraft) for real-time discussions
- Participate in our [Subreddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/admincraft/) community
- Report issues or suggest improvements on [GitHub](https://github.com/admincraft-community/admincraftwiki)

Whether you're here to learn, share knowledge, or find solutions, welcome to the Admincraft community!
