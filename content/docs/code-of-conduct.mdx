# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all community members, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, age, or religion.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or discriminatory behavior
- Inappropriate content or language
- Spamming or trolling
- Sharing others' private information
- Any form of harmful or disruptive conduct

<Callout type="info" title="Enforcement">
  Violations of these guidelines may result in temporary or permanent removal from the community. Moderators will enforce these standards fairly and consistently.
</Callout>

## Contact

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please contact the project team immediately. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
